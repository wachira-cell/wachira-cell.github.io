About Me

I am a passionate mathematics and physics teacher with over five years of experience teaching at the high school level. I hold a master of science degree in Applied mathematics from the university of Debrecen. My thesis focused on systems of ordinary equations allowing a given Lie group as their symmetry sub-group. I believe that strong mathematical foundations are essential for problem-solving, critical thinking, and informed decision-making in society.

Beyond the classroom, I have a strong interest in economics and public policy, particularly how economic decisions, data, and policy frameworks shape social and educational outcomes.
